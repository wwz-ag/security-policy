# **Responsible Disclosure Policy**

WWZ AG is committed to maintaining the security and reliability of its services and infrastructure. If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, we encourage you to report it to us responsibly.

 **Email:** [security@wwz.ch](mailto:security@wwz.ch)
 
## Please include where possible:
- affected service, system or URL
-	description of the vulnerability
-	reproduction steps
-	potential impact
-	optional proof-of-concept

## **Scope**
This policy applies to systems and services operated by WWZ AG and its affiliated companies, including:
-	WWZ Telekom AG
-	WWZ Energie AG
-	Telecom Cablenet AG
Testing systems of customers, partners or third parties is not permitted.


## **Conduct rules to be followed**
When conducting research, please:
-	do not breach any applicable laws in connection with your report and your interaction with WWZ’s product or service that lead to your report.
-	do not exploit or use in any manner the discovered vulnerability
-	only perform tests necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability
-	conduct security tests in good faith and for the purpose of improving the security of WWZ's IT systems
-	do not access, modify, delete, copy, retain, or exfiltrate any data; if any data becomes accessible unintentionally, stop immediately and report it to WWZ without delay and storing or sharing it
-	immediately stop testing if sensitive data becomes accessible or if such accessibility becomes foreseeable. 
-	do not perform denial-of-service or availability attacks or the like
-	do not use social engineering against employees or customers or any other persons
-	do not conduct automated large-scale scanning or the like
-	do not disclose to any third-party any information related to the vulnerability nor the fact that a vulnerability has been found
-	you agree that you are making your report without any expectation or requirement of reward or other benefit, financial or otherwise


## **Coordinated Disclosure**
WWZ follows a coordinated vulnerability disclosure process.

After receiving your report:
-	we will acknowledge receipt
-	we will validate the finding
-	we will work on remediation
-	we will keep you informed about progress

WWZ aims to remediate vulnerabilities within a reasonable timeframe.

Public disclosure must be coordinated with WWZ.

## **Safe Harbor**
If you act in good faith, follow this policy and no applicable law has been infringed:
-	WWZ will not initiate legal action against you
-	WWZ will not pursue claims under applicable computer misuse laws
-	WWZ considers your research authorised within the scope of this policy
-	WWZ will treat your report confidentially
-	your identity will not be shared without your permission unless legally required

This safe harbor applies only to activities conducted within the scope and rules of this policy.

## **Rewards**
WWZ does not operate a public bug bounty program. However, we value responsible disclosure and may acknowledge valid reports at our discretion.

## **Communication**
Please allow reasonable time for investigation and remediation before any public disclosure. We encourage cooperative and transparent communication throughout the process.

## **Personal Data**
WWZ treats persaonal data in accordance with its data protection declaration (wwz.ch/datenschutz).
